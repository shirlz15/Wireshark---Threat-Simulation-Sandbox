Detection Rules (Priority Order)
CRITICAL · PORT_SCAN — 12+ unique ports in 30s window → confidence 60–99%
CRITICAL · BRUTE_FORCE — 6+ hits on critical port in 10s → confidence 55–98%
CRITICAL · TRAFFIC_FLOOD — 25+ packets in 5s from single IP → confidence 50–97%
SUSPICIOUS · RECON_SEQUENCE — wide scan → sensitive port pivot (behavioral sequence)
SUSPICIOUS · SENSITIVE_PORT — repeated access to DB/admin service
SUSPICIOUS · PORT_SPREAD — 4+ ports in 10s (enumeration pattern)
SAFE · NORMAL — no anomalous pattern detected
